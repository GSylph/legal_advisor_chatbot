# Legal Advisor Chatbot 

The Legal Advisor Chatbot for [COUNTRY] is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to offer accessible, preliminary legal guidance to users on matters related to:

1. Property law
2. Land ownership
3. Property disputes
4. Tenant and landlord rights

Users can interact with the chatbot in natural language to ask questions, understand their rights, and receive step-by-step suggestions or warnings about actions to take during legal processes and disputes.

This chatbot does not replace legal counsel, but aims to provide:

- Informative insights based on local laws and regulations
- Early-stage guidance during legal conflicts
- Awareness of legal procedures and responsibilities

# Key Features:
- Escalation advice (e.g., when to contact a lawyer or file a complaint)
- Interactive Q&A with follow-up suggestions
- Knowledge base aligned with country's legal code


# Use Cases:
- "Can my landlord evict me without notice?"
- "What documents do I need to prove land ownership?"
- "There is a boundary dispute with my neighbor — what can I do?"
- "Is oral agreement valid in tenancy?"
